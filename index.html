About Me






Hi there! I'm Siliziwe Pani

Web Developer

Are you looking for awesomeness? Look here. Not there. Do you want to know me? Come closer so I can whisper my attitude. I can resist anything but temptation. Sometimes, it takes me all day to get nothing done.I'm not lazy; I just run on energy-saving mode!
I do nothing every day, but people keep saying nothing is impossible. All this time, I thought I wanted a job. Turns out, I just wanted a paycheck. I give my friends admirable advice sometimes, but I'm not sure I'm capable of taking them.
This is who I am. Nobody said you had to like it. I'm hoping you find my awkwardness cute rather than weird.
When I'm wrong, I'm the worst. When I'm good, I'm the best. My success is just postponed for some time. I have not failed yet.
